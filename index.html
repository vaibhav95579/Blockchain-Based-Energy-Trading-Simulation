Introduction

With the growth of decentralized energy systems, there is a critical need for safe, efficient, and transparent platforms to facilitate energy trade. Our framework addresses these needs by leveraging blockchain technology to enable peer-to-peer energy trading while tackling scalability, privacy, and cost challenges.

Key Features

Dual-Layer Architecture

Combines private channels for sensitive data with a public ledger for transparency and auditability.

Proof-of-Stake Consensus

Energy-efficient and scalable, supporting a higher transaction throughput.

Dynamic Pricing Algorithm

Ensures fair and real-time pricing based on demand and supply conditions.

Enhanced Security

Multi-signature wallets and end-to-end encryption for secure transactions.

Energy Forecasting

Leverages AI to predict energy demand and optimize trading strategies.

Decentralized Governance

Implements DAOs to manage the network and ensure democratic decision-making.

Implementation

Our simulation environment tested the framework with 1,000 prosumers under dynamic demand and fluctuating supply conditions. Results showed significant improvements in scalability, privacy, and cost efficiency compared to existing systems.

  • Transaction throughput: 2,000 transactions per second.
  • Energy consumption reduced by 85% compared to PoW systems.
  • Operational costs lowered by 70%.
